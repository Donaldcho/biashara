# Biashara AI Pro User Manual

**For business owners, managers, and staff using Biashara AI Pro**  
**Documentation date:** 2026-05-20

Biashara AI Pro helps you run a product and service business from your phone. You can record sales, manage stock, sell services, issue prepaid vouchers, track customer credit, view your ledger, and ask the built-in AI assistant questions about your business.

Most daily work does not need internet. Your sales, stock, customers, ledger, services, vouchers, and chat history are stored on the device. Full AI features need the AI model downloaded on the phone.

## What Pro Adds

Pro includes all product-shop features plus:

- Services catalogue.
- Sales mode for Products, Services, or Both.
- Mixed product and service cart.
- Staff picker for service delivery.
- Prepaid service vouchers.
- Service catalogue QR codes.
- Service receipt/warranty QR verification.
- Mixed payment plans for products and services.
- Deposit and balance-due sales.
- Service revenue in receipts, ledger, reports, Today, and weekly review.
- Pro service agents for utilisation, no-shows, service pricing, and voucher expiry.
- Staff settings.

Basic/Shop remains product-only. If you do not see Services in Inventory or Sales, check your Pro licence in Settings.

## First-Time Setup

### 1. Choose language

On first launch, select the language you want to use. The app supports English and several African languages/locales. You can continue using the app offline after setup.

### 2. Check your edition

Open **Settings** and look at the licence area.

You should see:

- Product line: Pro.
- Edition: Private, SME, or Enterprise depending on your key.
- Maximum devices allowed by the key.

If it still shows Shop or invalid:

1. Paste your Pro licence key.
2. Tap **Apply**.
3. Return to Inventory and confirm the Services tab appears.

### 3. Set currency

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **Currency**.
3. Choose your local currency.

All prices, receipts, ledger entries, and reports use this setting.

### 4. Add business profile

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **Business profile**.
3. Fill in your business name, owner name, products, services, location, customers, suppliers, hours, and goals.
4. Save.

Why this matters:

- Receipts show the correct business name.
- Chat and AI agents give advice in the context of your real business.
- Weekly review can mention your actual goals and services.

### 5. Download the AI model

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Look at **Device capability**.
3. If your device supports AI, tap **Download model**.
4. Use Wi-Fi and keep the phone charged.

The app works without the model, but some summaries and chat answers become rule-based and shorter.

### 6. Add staff

Pro can attach staff names to service sales.

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **Staff settings**.
3. Add owner, manager, or staff members.
4. Keep inactive staff disabled instead of reusing names.

When staff exists, selling a service can ask who performed it.

### 7. Set tax and receipt details

In Settings, configure:

- Business name.
- Tax rate if your business uses tax.
- Receipt footer.
- Printer settings if you use Bluetooth printing.
- Voice and TTS preferences if you want voice input or spoken alerts.

## Today Screen

Today is your daily command screen.

You see:

- A greeting with your business name.
- The date.
- An attention label showing how many cards need review.
- A brief summary from recent business data.
- Agent cards.
- Shortcuts like **Prepare supplier visit** and **Business ledger**.

### Using agent cards

Each card is one suggestion or warning.

Actions:

| Button | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Review/View | Open the card details or related screen. |
| Approve | Accept or execute the suggestion when supported. |
| Reject | Decline the suggestion. |
| Snooze | Hide it until later. |
| Dismiss | Remove the card. |
| Thumbs up | Mark advice helpful. |
| Thumbs down | Mark advice not helpful and hide similar repeated reports. |

If the AI keeps repeating the same information, use thumbs down on that card. The app stores this feedback and suppresses similar repeated reports for a period.

### Refreshing Today

Pull down on the Today list to run available agents again. Some heavy AI agents only run when the model is available and the phone has enough battery/charging.

### Prepare supplier visit

Use this before buying stock:

1. Tap **Prepare supplier visit**.
2. Select the products you plan to buy.
3. Let the app prepare a negotiation guide.
4. Use the guide when talking to the supplier.

This works best when inventory and cost prices are up to date.

### Business ledger button

Tap **Business ledger** to open Insights and Ledger. Use it for:

- Cash flow.
- Credit owed by customers.
- Ledger entries.
- Cash count.
- Manual ledger entry.

## Inventory

Inventory has Products for all users and Services for Pro.

### Products tab

Use Products for physical stock.

Product fields:

- Name.
- Description.
- Selling price.
- Cost price.
- Stock quantity.
- Category.
- Barcode.
- Photo/image.

Add a product:

1. Open **Inventory**.
2. Choose **Products**.
3. Tap **+**.
4. Enter product details.
5. Save.

Scan a product barcode:

1. Open the product form or scanner.
2. Tap scan.
3. Point the camera at the barcode.
4. Save the barcode to the product or add the product to sale.

Use cost price carefully. Profit, pricing suggestions, and margin depend on it.

### Services tab

Use Services for labor or appointments such as:

- Haircut.
- Braiding.
- Phone repair.
- Delivery.
- Consultation.
- Installation.
- Cleaning.

Add a service:

1. Open **Inventory**.
2. Tap **Services**.
3. Tap **+**.
4. Enter service name.
5. Enter price.
6. Enter duration in minutes if useful.
7. Enter warranty days if you offer a warranty.
8. Save.

After saving, the app can show a service catalogue QR token. You can copy it, preview it, or print it.

Edit a service:

1. Open Inventory.
2. Tap Services.
3. Tap the service.
4. Change details.
5. Save.

Delete a service only when it is not needed anymore. For old services with history, prefer renaming or hiding if the app provides that option, because past receipts still need their historical meaning.

## Sales POS

Open **Sales** to record a sale.

In Pro, you can switch the catalogue mode:

- Products.
- Services.
- Both.

Use Products when selling stock. Use Services for labor/session sales. Use Both when the customer buys items and services together.

### Product sale

1. Open **Sales**.
2. Set mode to Products or Both.
3. Search, scroll, or scan barcode.
4. Tap the product to add it to the cart.
5. Tap again or edit quantity to increase quantity.
6. Select a customer if needed.
7. Tap **Pay**.
8. Choose payment method.
9. Confirm sale.
10. Share or print receipt.

The app reduces stock automatically after the sale is committed.

### Service sale

1. Open **Sales**.
2. Set mode to Services or Both.
3. Tap the service.
4. Choose staff if asked.
5. Confirm the line in the cart.
6. Select a customer if needed.
7. Tap **Pay**.
8. Choose payment method.
9. Confirm sale.
10. Give the customer the receipt.

The app records a service delivery row for each service unit sold.

### Mixed product and service sale

Example: A salon sells hair product plus braiding service.

1. Open Sales.
2. Set mode to Both.
3. Add product lines.
4. Add service lines.
5. Attach customer if needed.
6. Tap Pay.
7. Choose how payment is handled.
8. Confirm sale.

The receipt will show product lines, service lines, subtotal, tax, paid amount, balance due if any, and total.

### Price override

If price override is enabled in Settings, you can change a line price for that sale only.

Use this for:

- Discount.
- Negotiated service price.
- Damaged item discount.
- Special customer agreement.

Be careful. Large discounts can affect profit and may trigger a warning.

## Customers

Use customers when you want to track:

- Credit.
- Deposits.
- Balance due.
- Repeat visits.
- Voucher ownership.
- Service history.

Add customer:

1. In Sales, tap the customer chip.
2. Search existing customer.
3. If not found, add name and phone.
4. Select the customer for the sale.

For walk-in cash sales, a customer is optional.

## Payments

Tap **Pay** after building the cart.

### Cash

Use when the customer pays cash.

1. Choose Cash.
2. Enter amount tendered if you want change calculated.
3. Confirm.

Receipt can show amount tendered and change.

### Mobile money

Use for M-Pesa, MTN, Airtel Money, Orange Money, or similar.

1. Choose Mobile money.
2. Select network if shown.
3. Enter or paste reference.
4. Confirm.

If the AI model is available, the app may help extract amount/reference from pasted SMS text.

### Split payment

Use when the customer pays part cash and part mobile money.

1. Enable split payment.
2. Choose first method and amount.
3. Choose second method and amount.
4. Confirm totals.
5. Complete sale.

### Credit

Use when the customer will pay later.

Credit requires a customer.

1. Select customer in Sales.
2. Tap Pay.
3. Choose Credit.
4. Add due date and note if needed.
5. Confirm.

The app creates a debt record and ledger entry.

### Deposit and balance due

Use when the customer pays part now and the rest later.

1. Select customer.
2. Build cart.
3. Tap Pay.
4. Choose Deposit plan.
5. Enter deposit amount.
6. Confirm.

The receipt shows paid now and balance due. Later, use the customer/sale balance collection flow to settle the remaining amount.

### Credit products or services only

For mixed carts, Pro can separate product and service payment responsibility.

Examples:

- Customer pays for product now but service is on credit.
- Customer pays for service now but product is on credit.

Choose the correct mixed payment plan in the payment dialog.

### Voucher payment

Use when a customer redeems a prepaid service voucher.

1. Choose Voucher payment or scan the voucher token.
2. Confirm the voucher is active.
3. Complete the redemption.

The voucher remaining uses decrease after the sale.

## Prepaid Service Vouchers

Vouchers are used for prepaid visits or sessions.

Example:

- Customer buys 5 haircut sessions.
- Customer returns later and redeems one session each visit.

### Sell a voucher

1. Open Sales.
2. Set mode to Services or Both.
3. Long-press the service.
4. Choose voucher/package options.
5. Select customer if required.
6. Add voucher to cart.
7. Tap Pay.
8. Complete payment.
9. Open voucher QR/card from the receipt.
10. Share, print, or show the voucher to the customer.

### Redeem a voucher

1. Open Sales.
2. Scan the voucher QR/barcode or choose Voucher payment.
3. Confirm the customer/service.
4. Complete sale or redemption.

The app records a neutral ledger event for voucher redemption and reduces remaining uses.

### Voucher expiry

Vouchers have an expiry date. The voucher expiry agent can warn you about vouchers that will expire soon.

## Service Receipts And Warranty

For services with warranty days, each delivery can have warranty information.

Use service receipt tokens to verify:

- Which service was delivered.
- When it was delivered.
- Whether warranty is still valid.

Scan a service receipt QR when a customer returns for warranty or proof of service.

If warranty work creates a cost, the app can record a warranty claim ledger entry.

## Receipts

After each successful sale, the receipt screen appears.

Receipts can include:

- Business name.
- Receipt number.
- Date/time.
- Payment method.
- Mobile money reference.
- Customer.
- Products.
- Services.
- Vouchers.
- Subtotal.
- Tax.
- Paid now.
- Balance due.
- Grand total.
- Footer.

Actions:

- Share receipt.
- Print receipt if printer is configured.
- View prepaid voucher QR if voucher was sold.
- Return product items if needed.

## Product Returns

Use returns when a customer brings back a physical product.

1. Open transaction history.
2. Open the original receipt.
3. Tap Return.
4. Select product lines and quantities.
5. Confirm.

The app creates a return transaction, restores stock, and records a refund ledger entry.

Current limitation: service refund/return is not yet a complete first-class workflow. Handle service corrections carefully through ledger adjustments or operational notes.

## Insights And Ledger

Open Insights from:

- Today -> Business ledger.
- Settings -> Open ledger.

Insights contains cash flow, credit, and ledger views.

### Cash flow

Use cash flow to see:

- Money in.
- Money out.
- Net cash flow.
- Income and expense categories.
- Period summaries.

### Credit

Use Credit to see:

- Customers who owe money.
- Amount owed.
- Due dates.
- Repayments.

Follow up with customers regularly. Credit only stays useful if every sale and repayment is recorded.

### Ledger

The ledger is the complete money record.

It includes:

- Product sales.
- Service sales.
- Mixed sales.
- Voucher sales.
- Voucher redemptions.
- Credit extended.
- Debt repaid.
- Refunds.
- Expenses.
- Stock purchases.
- Cash counts.
- Warranty claims.
- Manual adjustments.

The ledger is append-only. If you make a mistake, add a correction entry instead of trying to erase history.

### Manual ledger entry

Use manual entries for business money movement not captured by POS.

Examples:

- Rent payment.
- Transport cost.
- Supplier expense.
- Owner cash injection.
- Miscellaneous income.

### Cash count

Use cash count at end of day:

1. Count physical cash.
2. Open Ledger -> Cash count.
3. Enter actual cash.
4. Save.

The app compares expected ledger balance with actual cash and stores the difference.

## Chat

Open **Chat** to ask questions in plain language.

Examples:

- What did I sell today?
- Which products are low on stock?
- How much service revenue did I make this week?
- Who owes me money?
- Which service is most popular?
- What should I buy before visiting supplier?
- Did services beat product sales this week?

Chat uses local business data first. When AI is available, it can reword and explain answers more naturally.

### Chat sessions

The app keeps chat conversations as sessions. Open chat history to return to previous sessions or delete old ones.

### Copy, edit, and delete messages

Long-press a chat message to open message actions.

You can:

- Copy message text.
- Edit a message.
- Delete a message.

Editing changes the saved message text. Deleting removes that message from the session.

### Feedback on assistant replies

Use helpful/not-helpful feedback on assistant answers. This helps identify poor answers and supports better repeated behavior over time.

## AI Memory And Repeated Advice

Pro does not fine-tune the AI model on your phone. Instead, it improves context.

The app can remember:

- Weekly KPI snapshots.
- Revenue trends.
- Top products and services.
- Service revenue compared with product revenue.
- Forecast errors.
- Your goals mentioned in chat.
- Your preferences mentioned in chat.
- Feedback on repeated agent advice.

This is why recording real sales and using feedback buttons matters.

If the app repeats the same advice:

1. Tap thumbs down on the repeated Today card.
2. Dismiss it.
3. Keep recording sales normally.
4. Wait for future agent runs to use the feedback.

Common agents:

Keep your business profile updated when your business changes.

Update it when:

- You add a new service line.
- You change location.
- Your target customers change.
- You get new suppliers.
- You set a new monthly goal.
- Your opening hours change.

AI advice is better when this profile is accurate.

## Supplier Visit Workflow

Before going to supplier:

1. Update stock quantities.
2. Check low-stock cards on Today.
3. Open Prepare supplier visit.
4. Select products you need.
5. Review suggested negotiation points.
6. Record stock purchases or scan supplier receipt after buying.

After returning:

1. Update product stock.
2. Add new products if needed.
3. Record supplier expenses in ledger if not already captured.

## Receipt Scanning And Stock Intake

Use receipt scan when you buy stock and want to capture invoice lines.

1. Open Inventory.
2. Choose scan receipt.
3. Photograph the supplier receipt/invoice.
4. Review extracted lines.
5. Correct names, quantities, and costs.
6. Save to inventory.

Always review OCR output. Receipts can be unclear, and the app may misread numbers or product names.

## WhatsApp And Shared Orders

The app supports local Android WhatsApp helper flows.

You can:

- Open WhatsApp from Settings if installed.
- Copy order text from WhatsApp and parse it from clipboard.
- Share text into the order parser when Android offers Biashara AI as a target.

This is not the same as automatic WhatsApp Business cloud integration. For Pro, treat it as a phone-based helper for shared text and manual workflows.

## Staff Management

Use staff records to track who performed services.

Good practice:

- Add each staff member separately.
- Use real names or consistent nicknames.
- Deactivate old staff instead of reusing their record.
- Use manager/owner roles only for trusted people.
- Use PINs if your workflow requires operator confirmation.

Service receipts and ledger notes can include staff names.

### Morning

1. Open Today.
2. Review urgent cards.
3. Check stock warnings.
4. Check voucher expiry or service cards.
5. Prepare supplier visit if buying stock today.

### During sales

1. Record every sale in Sales.
2. Use customer names for credit, deposits, services, and vouchers.
3. Use correct payment method.
4. Share or print receipts when needed.
5. Keep product/service prices accurate.

### Afternoon or slow period

1. Add new stock.
2. Update services.
3. Review customer debts.
4. Ask Chat for performance questions.

### End of day

1. Review transaction history.
2. Open Ledger.
3. Record expenses not already captured.
4. Do cash count if you handle cash.
5. Check credit due soon.

## Weekly Routine

1. Keep the phone charged so weekly AI review can run.
2. Open Today and read weekly review.
3. Check top product and top service.
4. Compare product vs service revenue.
5. Review outstanding credit.
6. Decide what to restock.
7. Adjust service pricing if the service pricing agent gives a useful recommendation.
8. Give thumbs up/down feedback on advice.

## Troubleshooting

### I do not see Services

Check:

- Pro licence is applied in Settings.
- Licence says Product line: Pro.
- Restart app after applying key if UI did not refresh.

### AI answers are slow

Possible causes:

- Model is still downloading.
- Device is low RAM.
- Battery is low.
- Heavy weekly review is running.
- Prompt is long.

Use rule-based features while waiting. Sales and ledger do not need the model.

### AI gives repeated advice

Use thumbs down on the repeated card. The app will store feedback and hide similar rejected reports for a period.

### Sale failed because stock is not enough

The app checks stock at commit time. Update stock quantity or reduce cart quantity.

### Credit sale is blocked

Credit requires a customer. Select or create a customer before confirming.

### Voucher will not redeem

Check:

- Voucher ID is correct.
- Voucher has remaining uses.
- Voucher is not expired.
- The QR/barcode was scanned clearly.

### Receipt is missing voucher QR

Open the sale receipt and look for the voucher QR/button. If the voucher was not sold in that transaction, open the voucher record/card from its voucher ID if available.

### Printer does not work

Check:

- Bluetooth is on.
- Printer is paired with Android.
- Printer has paper.
- Correct paper width is set.
- Battery is charged.

You can still share receipts digitally.

### Chat message needs correction

Long-press the message and choose Edit. If the message should not remain in the thread, choose Delete.

### Ledger balance looks wrong

Check:

- Recent manual entries.
- Refunds.
- Credit repayments.
- Cash counts.
- Duplicate expense entries.

Use adjustment entries for corrections. Do not clear app data.

## Data And Privacy

For normal Pro use:

- Data stays on the phone.
- AI runs on the phone after model download.
- Sales, services, vouchers, customers, debts, ledger, and chat history are local.
- Sharing receipts or reports is your choice.

Protect the phone with a device lock. If the phone is lost and there is no backup/export, local business data may be lost.

## Good Data Habits

The AI and reports are only as good as the data you record.

Best practice:

- Record every sale.
- Use correct payment method.
- Add customers for credit and vouchers.
- Keep stock quantities current.
- Enter cost prices.
- Record expenses.
- Use cash count daily if you handle cash.
- Mark bad AI advice with thumbs down.
- Keep business profile updated.
