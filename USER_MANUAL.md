# Biashara AI — User Manual

**For shop and service business owners in Africa**

Biashara AI helps you run your business from your phone: record sales, track stock, manage credit, understand cash flow, and get smart suggestions from built-in AI agents. Your data stays on your device unless you choose to export or use optional cloud features.

## 1. Who this app is for

Biashara AI is built for small businesses such as:

- Retail shops (*dukas*), kiosks, and wholesalers  
- Salons, barbers, and beauty services  
- Mixed businesses that sell products **and** offer services  

You do not need a computer or constant internet. Core features work offline after setup. Full AI features need a one-time download of the language model (about 2.5 GB, best on Wi‑Fi).

## 2. Getting started

### First launch

1. **Choose your language** — English, Kiswahili, Hausa, Yorùbá, or አማርኛ (Amharic). The whole app uses the language you pick.
2. You land on **Today**, your home screen for AI suggestions.

### Tell the app about your business (recommended)

Agents and Chat work much better when they know *your* story, not just your numbers.

1. Open **Settings** → **Business profile**.
2. Either:
   - **Redo conversation setup** — answer eight short questions one at a time (business name, what you offer, customers, hours, location, your name, suppliers, goals), or  
   - Edit the fields directly and tap **Save profile**.

Examples of what helps: *“Amina’s Hair Studio — natural hair, braiding and locs; young professionals near Westlands; open Tuesday–Sunday; supplier Mama Grace in Gikomba.”*

### Download the AI model (optional but recommended)

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Under **AI Model**, check your device tier:
   - **Full AI** — Chat, weekly reviews, and rich agent messages.
   - **Partial AI** — Shorter agent messages; some features use rules only.
   - **Rules only** — Alerts and numbers without Gemma narratives.
3. Tap **Download model** when on Wi‑Fi. Wait until status shows ready.

### Set your currency

**Settings** → **Regional** → choose your currency (KES, NGN, TZS, UGX, GHS, ZAR, ETB, and many other African currencies).

### Activate a Pro licence (if you have one)

**Settings** → paste your licence key → **Apply**. Pro unlocks services, vouchers, staff, and service-specific agents (see [Shop vs Pro](#10-shop-vs-pro)).

## 4. Today — your AI home screen

**Today** is where background **agents** post suggestions. They watch your sales, stock, ledger, and (on Pro) appointments and services while you work.

### What you see

- **Greeting** with your business name and the date.
- **Attention chip** — how many actions need your review (or “all caught up”).
- **Action cards** — each card is one suggestion (stock low, pricing idea, cash summary, customer SMS draft, weekly review, etc.).

### What you can do on a card

| Action | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Review** / **View** | Open details |
| **Approve** | Accept (e.g. send an SMS you approved) |
| **Reject** | Decline this suggestion |
| **Snooze** | Remind me later |
| **Dismiss** | Remove from the list |

Critical alerts may be **read aloud** if you turned on voice in Settings.

### Shortcuts on Today

- **Prepare supplier visit** — builds a negotiation script from items you pick (needs Full AI + downloaded model).
- **Business ledger** — opens Insights → Ledger.

### Pull down to refresh

Triggers agents to check your shop again.

## 5. Inventory — products and services

### Products tab (all editions)

**See your catalogue** — name, stock level, photo, barcode indicator.

**Add products**

- Tap **+** → **Add manually**, **Scan barcode**, or **Scan receipt** (photograph a supplier invoice and import lines).
- On the product form you can set: name, description, sell price, cost price, stock quantity, category, photo, barcode.
- **Scan label** — point camera at packaging to read the name.
- **Voice** — speak the product name when adding.
- **Suggest price** — AI suggestion based on cost (when model is available).

**Manage existing products**

- Tap a product to **edit**.
- Long-press or menu: **Remove from stock** (adjust quantity) or **Delete product**.

**Empty shop?** The screen prompts you to add your first product.

### Services tab (Pro only)

If you have a Pro licence, a **Services** tab appears for salons, clinics, repair shops, etc.

- Add services: name, price, duration, warranty days.
- Each service can have a **catalogue QR** for customers.
- If the tab is missing, apply a Pro licence in Settings.

### Inventory menu

- **Prepare supplier visit** — same negotiation tool as on Today.

## 6. Sales — point of sale (POS)

**Sales** is where every transaction is recorded.

### Catalog mode (Pro)

Toggle **Products**, **Services**, or **Both** at the top when you sell mixed goods and labour.

### Building a sale — products

1. Search or scroll the product grid; tap to add (tap again to increase quantity).
2. **Scan barcode** to add by code.
3. Optional: tap **Customer** to attach a buyer (needed for credit).
4. Review **cart** — subtotal, tax, total.
5. Tap **Pay**.

### Building a sale — services (Pro)

1. Tap a service on the grid.
2. If asked, **pick staff** or skip.
3. **Long-press a service** to sell a **prepaid voucher** (package of visits).

### Customer

- **Walk-in** — no name required for simple cash sales.
- **Existing customer** — search and select.
- **New customer** — add name and phone.
- **Credit**, **deposit**, and **balance** sales require a customer.

### Payment

When you tap **Pay**, choose:

| Method | Use when |
|--------|----------|
| **Cash** | Customer pays cash; enter amount tendered for change |
| **Mobile money** | M-Pesa, Airtel Money, etc. |
| **Split payment** | Part cash, part mobile (two amounts) |
| **On credit** | Customer pays later; set due date and note |
| **Voucher** (Pro) | Customer redeems prepaid sessions |

**Pro — mixed cart (products + services)**

- **Payment plan** may offer: pay everything now, credit products only, credit services only, or **deposit now + balance later**.
- After a deposit sale, **long-press the customer chip** on Sales to **collect balance** when they return.

### After payment

- View **receipt** — share or print if you use a Bluetooth printer.
- **Return items** from the receipt screen if the customer brings goods back.
- **Voucher sold** (Pro) — show QR/barcode card for the customer to keep.

### Sales menu (⋮)

- **Transaction history** — past sales; open any receipt.
- **Close day** — end-of-day totals, top item, payment mix; share summary.

### Scan at POS (Pro)

- **Scan service / voucher** — redeem a session from the customer’s QR.

## 7. Insights — cash flow, credit, and ledger

Open via **Today → Business ledger** or **Settings → Open ledger**.

### Cash flow tab

- Chart of **money in** vs **money out**.
- **Net position** and a short **AI narrative** about how the period looks (when Full AI is available).
- Pull to **refresh**.

### Credit tab

Track **informal credit** (customers who owe you).

- See **total outstanding** and each debt (amount, days overdue).
- **Remind** — preview an SMS and open your phone’s SMS app to send.
- **Paid** — mark the debt settled when they pay.

Credit is also created automatically when you sell **on credit** at POS.

### Ledger tab

Your **running business ledger** — every cash movement in and out.

- **Balance**, totals in, totals out.
- Search entries.
- **+** button (FAB) options:
  - **Manual entry** — record money in or out by hand.
  - **Cash count** — reconcile physical cash in the drawer vs expected.
  - **Export CSV** — spreadsheet for your records or accountant.
  - **Scan proof** — photograph M-Pesa slip or bank proof.
  - **Paste M-Pesa SMS** — paste confirmation text from SMS.
  - **QR category cards** — print shortcuts for fast cash capture.

**Toolbar** — **Transactions** opens the same sales history as Sales → menu.

## 8. Chat — ask your business assistant

Type or speak questions in everyday language.

### Example questions

- “What were today’s sales?”
- “What is my profit this week?”
- “Which products are low on stock?”
- “Who owes me money?”
- “What were my top expenses this month?”

### Features

- **Suggested prompts** at the top for common questions.
- **Microphone** — voice input (enable in Settings).
- **Read aloud** — hear answers (text-to-speech).
- **Attach image** — photo of a receipt or stock.
- **New chat** / **History** — separate conversations; delete old ones from history.

### How answers work

- With the **AI model downloaded**, Chat uses your real shop data via tools (sales, inventory, ledger, customers).
- Without the model, you still get **structured answers** from your stored data where possible.
- Chat responds in your **app language**.

### Voice navigation (from Chat)

You can say things like “open sales”, “go to inventory”, or “show ledger” to jump to main screens (wording varies by language).

## 9. Settings

### AI Model

- See **device capability** (Full / Partial / Rules).
- **Download** or **delete** the Gemma model.
- Open **Model settings** for advanced options and benchmarks.

### Configurations

Fine-tune inference (temperature, tokens, CPU/GPU) — for advanced users.

### Cloud analysis (optional)

If you have a server endpoint, upload an anonymised business snapshot or database backup. Requires your own HTTPS URL and API key. **Off by default.**

### WhatsApp orders

- **Parse order from clipboard** — paste customer order text from WhatsApp.
- Or **share text** from WhatsApp into Biashara AI → **Order from message** → match products → record sale.

### Voice & speech

- Turn **voice input** on or off globally.
- **Voice settings**: Whisper model size, recognition language (Auto, English, Swahili, Amharic, Hausa, Yoruba), TTS speed, auto-read Chat answers and critical agent alerts.

### Business

- **Open ledger** — Insights.
- **Business profile** — edit or redo conversational setup (see [Getting started](#tell-the-app-about-your-business-recommended)).

### AI agents

- **Agent run history** — when each agent last ran; expand for log lines.
- Toggle agents **on/off** (master switch and per-agent switches on this screen).

### Staff members (Pro)

Add or deactivate staff names used when recording services at POS.

### Regional

Choose **currency** for all amounts and receipts.

### Licence

Paste a **licence key** from your supplier → **Apply**. Shows Shop or Pro, edition, and device limit.

### About

App version number.

## 10. Shop vs Pro

One app; features unlock with your **licence key**.

| Feature | Shop | Pro |
|---------|:----:|:---:|
| Products, POS, credit, ledger, Chat | ✓ | ✓ |
| Core agents (stock, pricing, cash flow, customers, weekly review, …) | ✓ | ✓ |
| Business profile | ✓ | ✓ |
| **Services** in Inventory | — | ✓ |
| Service mode on POS, staff picker | — | ✓ |
| **Prepaid vouchers** (packages) | — | ✓ |
| Mixed product + service payment plans | — | ✓ |
| Deposit + collect balance | — | ✓ |
| Staff settings | — | ✓ |
| Service agents (utilisation, no-show, service pricing, voucher expiry) | — | ✓ |

**Editions** on the key (Private, SME, Enterprise) mainly control deployment tier and how many devices may use the same key — shown in Settings after activation.

## 11. Your AI agents — what they do

Agents run in the background (usually daily or weekly). They never spend your money or change prices without you approving an action card.

| Agent | What it watches | What you get |
|-------|-----------------|--------------|
| **Stock Guardian** | Sales speed vs stock on hand | “Order X before you run out” |
| **Pricing agent** | Slow movers, margins, sales spikes | One-line pricing rationale per product |
| **Cash flow sentinel** | Today vs your 30-day average | Daily cash digest and one tip |
| **Customer relations** | Overdue credit, long gaps between visits | SMS drafts for reminders or “we miss you” |
| **Fraud Sentinel** | Odd transactions, cash evidence | Flags to review before loss grows |
| **Weekly review** | Full week of sales and credit | Friendly narrative: win, watch item, next step |
| **Opportunity spotter** | Products bought together | Bundle and shelf ideas |
| **Ledger anomaly** | Unusual ledger patterns | Large outflows, negative balance warnings |
| **Utilisation** (Pro) | Service bookings vs capacity | “Room to take more clients” or “near full” |
| **No-show tracker** (Pro) | Missed appointments | Suggests deposits for repeat no-shows |
| **Service pricing** (Pro) | Costs up, prices unchanged | Nudge to review service prices |
| **Voucher expiry** (Pro) | Unused prepaid sessions | Reminder before sessions expire |

Turn agents off in **Settings → Agent run history** if you do not want a particular type of alert.

## 12. Scanning, receipts, and vouchers

| What you scan | Where | Purpose |
|---------------|-------|---------|
| **Product barcode** | Inventory, Sales | Find or add product |
| **Supplier receipt** | Inventory → Scan receipt | Import many products from invoice photo |
| **Product label** | Add product | Read name from packaging |
| **Service / voucher QR** | Sales (Pro) | Redeem prepaid visit |
| **Cash proof** | Ledger → Scan | M-Pesa / bank slip → money in or out |
| **BSRC service receipt** | Verify screen | Check genuine service warranty QR |

### Three different “receipts”

1. **Supplier receipt scan** — stock *coming in* from wholesaler.  
2. **Sale receipt** — proof you gave the *customer* after POS.  
3. **Service receipt / voucher card** (Pro) — customer’s prepaid package QR.

Do not confuse them — each flow has its own button.

### Vouchers (Pro)

1. **Sell**: Sales → long-press service → set sessions, price, validity → customer gets QR/card.  
2. **Redeem**: Payment → Voucher tab, or scan at POS.  
3. **Track**: Remaining sessions; **Voucher expiry** agent warns before time runs out.

## 13. Voice

1. **Settings** → enable voice input.  
2. **Voice settings** → pick recognition language (or Automatic).  
3. Use the **microphone** in Chat, Sales search, or product name fields.  
4. Optional: **Read aloud** for Chat answers; **auto-read** critical agent alerts on Today.

Voice works best in a quiet place with a short, clear phrase.

## 14. Tips and troubleshooting

### “Agents still say ‘your business’ instead of my shop name”

Complete **Settings → Business profile**. Agents read that profile before they write messages.

### Model will not download

- Use **Wi‑Fi**; need several GB free storage.  
- Plug in power; retry from Settings.  
- Partial AI still gives useful rule-based alerts without the full model.

### Barcode will not scan

- Good lighting; hold steady.  
- Voucher barcodes need the printed **QR/token** from the voucher card, not only the internal ID.

### Credit customer not showing

Create or select the customer **before** choosing **On credit** at payment.

### Pro features missing

Apply a **Pro** licence key in Settings. Restart the app if the Services tab does not appear.

### Data and privacy

- Sales, stock, ledger, and profile data are stored **on your phone**.  
- Optional **cloud upload** and **export CSV** are under your control.  
- Back up by exporting ledger CSV and keeping your phone safe.

### Getting help inside the app

- **Chat** — “How do I add a product?” / “How does credit work?”  
- Contextual **lessons** may appear when you use a feature for the first time.  
- Agent cards often include a **Review** path with more detail.
