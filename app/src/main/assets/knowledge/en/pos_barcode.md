# Barcode Scanning at POS
feature_id: pos_barcode
language: en

Barcode scanning lets you add products to the cart instantly without searching — ideal for high-volume retail shops, supermarkets, and pharmacies.

## How to Scan a Barcode at POS

1. Open the **POS** screen.
2. Tap the **barcode icon** (camera symbol) near the search bar, or press the physical scan button if you have a Bluetooth barcode scanner paired.
3. Point your phone camera at the product barcode. Hold it steady 10–20 cm from the barcode.
4. The app reads the barcode and:
   - **If the product exists**: It is added to the cart immediately.
   - **If the product is not found**: You are prompted to create a new product with that barcode pre-filled.
5. Continue scanning additional items. Each scan adds one unit.
6. To add more than one unit of the same item, scan it multiple times or tap the cart item and change the quantity manually.

## Using a Bluetooth Barcode Scanner

1. Pair your Bluetooth scanner with your Android device via **Settings → Bluetooth**.
2. Open BiasharaAI. The app automatically detects hardware scanner input.
3. Scan barcodes — no camera needed. This is faster and works in bright sunlight.

## Supported Barcode Formats

- EAN-13, EAN-8 (European Article Number — most retail products)
- UPC-A, UPC-E (common in Kenya/Tanzania for imported goods)
- QR Code (can store a product ID)
- Code 128, Code 39 (often used on custom labels)

## Tips

- Print your own barcodes for products without a manufacturer barcode using the **Print Label** option in the product detail screen.
- If scanning is slow, ensure your camera lens is clean and there is adequate lighting.
- Scanning in continuous mode (hold the icon): tap once to toggle, scan multiple items, tap again to stop.
