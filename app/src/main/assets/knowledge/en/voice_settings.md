# Voice Settings
feature_id: voice_settings
language: en

Voice Settings let you choose how speech recognition and text-to-speech work in BiasharaAI. Go to **Settings → Voice Settings** to configure these options.

## Speech-to-Text (STT) Model

BiasharaAI uses an on-device STT model — your voice never leaves your phone.

- **Model**: The currently loaded STT model is shown here. Tap to change it if multiple models are downloaded.
- **Language Mode**:
  - **English only**: Fastest and most accurate if you only speak English.
  - **Swahili only**: Optimised for Swahili vocabulary including product names.
  - **Auto-detect (English + Swahili)**: The model detects the language automatically. Slightly slower but recommended for mixed-language speakers.
- **Download STT model**: If no model is installed, tap this to download one (requires Wi-Fi, approximately 150 MB).

## Microphone Input

- **Input Sensitivity**: Adjusts how loud you need to speak. Increase it in noisy market environments.
- **Silence Timeout**: How long (in seconds) the app waits after you stop speaking before finalising the transcript. Default is 1.5 seconds.
- **Wake Phrase**: Enable "Hey Biashara" to start recording without tapping the microphone icon. Toggle on and tap **Train** to record your voice for better accuracy.

## Text-to-Speech (TTS) Output

The app can read AI responses aloud — useful when your hands are busy or the screen is hard to see.

- **Enable TTS**: Toggle to have the AI's chat responses spoken aloud.
- **Voice**: Choose between available on-device voices (varies by Android version).
- **Speech Rate**: Slide between Slow, Normal, and Fast.
- **Language**: Match to your preferred language for the spoken output.

## Testing Your Settings

Tap **Test Microphone** to record a short phrase and see the transcription. Tap **Test TTS** to hear a sample sentence spoken aloud.
