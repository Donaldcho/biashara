# Make a POS Sale
feature_id: pos_sale
language: en

The POS (Point of Sale) screen is where you record every sale to a customer. It is fast, works offline, and automatically updates stock and the ledger.

## Steps to Make a Sale

1. Tap **POS** in the bottom navigation bar.
2. **Add items** to the cart:
   - Tap a product tile to add one unit.
   - Tap the product again to increase quantity, or swipe the cart item and enter a number directly.
   - Use the **Search** bar to find a product by name.
   - Tap the **barcode** icon to scan a product barcode.
3. **Adjust quantities or prices** (optional):
   - Tap a cart item to open the line editor.
   - Change quantity, apply a line discount, or override the unit price for this sale only.
4. **Select a customer** (optional but recommended for debt tracking):
   - Tap **Customer** at the top of the cart.
   - Search for an existing customer or tap **New Customer** to add one quickly.
5. **Apply a discount** (optional):
   - Tap **Discount** to add a percentage or fixed-amount discount to the entire sale.
6. **Choose payment method**:
   - **Cash**: Enter the amount tendered — the app calculates change automatically.
   - **M-Pesa / Mobile Money**: Enter the transaction reference number for records.
   - **Bank Transfer**: Enter the reference.
   - **Credit (Debt)**: Records the sale as unpaid — linked to the selected customer's debt account.
   - **Split Payment**: Combine two methods (e.g., part cash, part M-Pesa).
7. Tap **Charge** / **Complete Sale**.
8. **Receipt**: Choose to print (Bluetooth thermal printer), share via WhatsApp/SMS, or skip.

## Tips

- The POS works fully offline. Sales sync when you reconnect.
- You can add a tax rate per sale or configure a global tax in Settings.
- Tap the **Hold** button to park a sale and start another — useful when a customer is undecided.
