# Receipt Scanning
feature_id: receipt_scan
language: en

Receipt scanning uses your phone camera to capture supplier or expense receipts and automatically extract the data into BiasharaAI as an expense entry. This saves time and creates a digital paper trail.

## How to Scan a Receipt

1. Go to **Ledger** or **Expenses** and tap the **Scan Receipt** button (camera icon), or access it via the **+** button → **Scan Receipt**.
2. Point your camera at the receipt. Keep it flat, well-lit, and fully in the frame.
3. Tap the **capture** button. The app processes the image on-device.
4. The AI extracts:
   - **Vendor / Supplier name**
   - **Date**
   - **Total amount**
   - **Line items** (if the receipt is clear enough)
   - **VAT / Tax amount** (if shown)
5. Review the extracted data. Tap any field to correct it if the extraction was inaccurate.
6. Assign a **Category** (e.g., Stock Purchase, Rent, Transport, Utilities).
7. Tap **Save** to record the expense.

## Supported Receipt Types

- Handwritten receipts: Partially supported — printed text is more accurate.
- Thermal printer receipts: Best results.
- Supplier invoice slips.
- Supermarket or wholesale receipts (Tuskys, Naivas, Carrefour).
- M-Pesa transaction receipts (paper or screenshot).

## Tips for Best Results

- Place the receipt on a dark, flat surface for contrast.
- Ensure the receipt is not crumpled or folded — flatten it first.
- Avoid shadows across the receipt.
- If a receipt is too faded, enter the data manually using **+ New Expense**.

## Viewing Scanned Receipts

Each expense recorded via scan stores the original receipt image. To view it:
1. Go to **Ledger → Expenses**.
2. Tap any expense that was scanned.
3. Tap the **image thumbnail** to see the full receipt photo.

## Manual Expense Entry

If scanning does not work well, tap **+ New Expense** to type the details manually. All fields are the same.
