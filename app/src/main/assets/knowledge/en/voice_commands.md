# Voice Commands
feature_id: voice_commands
language: en

Voice commands let you operate BiasharaAI hands-free using spoken language. Speak in English or Swahili — the app understands both.

## Starting Voice Input

- Tap the **microphone** icon on any screen that supports it (Chat, POS, product entry).
- Or say the wake phrase (if enabled in Voice Settings): **"Hey Biashara"**.
- Speak clearly at a normal pace. The app transcribes your speech and executes the command or query.

## Supported Voice Commands

### Sales and POS
| Say | Action |
|-----|--------|
| "Add two Coca-Cola" | Adds 2 units of Coca-Cola to the POS cart |
| "Sell one kilo of unga" | Adds 1 kg of flour to the cart |
| "Charge cash" | Completes the sale with cash payment |
| "New sale" | Clears the cart and starts a new sale |
| "Apply discount ten percent" | Applies a 10% discount |

### Queries
| Say | Action |
|-----|--------|
| "What are my sales today?" | Shows today's revenue |
| "How much does John owe?" | Shows John's outstanding debt |
| "Stock of sugar" | Shows current stock level for sugar |

### Swahili Commands
| Sema | Kitendo |
|------|---------|
| "Ongeza soda mbili" | Inaongeza soda 2 kwenye mkoba wa mauzo |
| "Mauzo ya leo ni ngapi?" | Inaonyesha jumla ya mauzo ya leo |
| "Deni ya Amina" | Inaonyesha deni ya mteja Amina |
| "Malipo ya pesa taslimu" | Inakamilisha mauzo kwa pesa taslimu |

## Data Entry by Voice

In product forms and customer forms, tap the microphone icon next to any text field to dictate that field's value. For example, tap the microphone next to the Name field and say "Unga wa Pembe" to fill it in.

## Tips

- Speak product names exactly as you named them in the app.
- Numerals work in both words and digits: "two" and "2" are both understood.
- If the transcription is wrong, tap the text to edit before confirming.
