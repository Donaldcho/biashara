# AI Agent Feed
feature_id: agent_feed
language: en

The Agent Feed is your AI-powered business assistant. It monitors your business data continuously and surfaces actionable suggestions — without you having to ask.

## What the Agent Feed Does

The AI agent runs quietly in the background and generates **Action Cards** when it notices something important. Examples include:

- "Stock of Unga is at 2 bags — reorder soon."
- "Customer Jane has not paid her KES 1,500 debt in 14 days."
- "Your Tuesday sales are consistently 40% lower — consider a promotion."
- "You made KES 5,200 profit this week — up 12% from last week."

## Viewing the Agent Feed

1. Tap **Agent** in the bottom navigation bar.
2. Cards appear in a feed, newest at the top.
3. Each card shows: the suggestion, the reason behind it, and available actions.

## Responding to an Action Card

Each card has one or more buttons:

- **Approve**: The app executes the suggested action (e.g., sends a debt reminder, creates a stock alert).
- **Dismiss**: Removes the card. You can recover dismissed cards from the archive.
- **Snooze**: Hides the card and resurfaces it after a set time (e.g., "Remind me tomorrow").
- **View Details**: Opens the related screen (e.g., the customer's debt profile).

## Agent Types

- **Cash Flow Sentinel**: Monitors debts and cash flow.
- **Stock Guardian**: Tracks inventory levels and warns about low or excess stock.
- **Sales Analyst**: Identifies trends, anomalies, and growth opportunities.
- **Expense Watcher**: Flags unusual expense spikes.

## Customising the Agent Feed

Go to **Settings → Agent Settings** to enable or disable individual agents, set quiet hours, and adjust alert thresholds.

## Notes

- The agent runs on-device using the downloaded AI model — no internet connection is required.
- Agents only produce cards when they have enough data (at least a few days of sales history).
