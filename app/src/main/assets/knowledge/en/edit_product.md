# Edit / Update a Product
feature_id: edit_product
language: en

You can update any product detail at any time. Edits apply going forward and do not change historical sales records.

## Steps to Edit a Product

1. Open the **Products** screen.
2. Find the product by scrolling or using the **Search** bar at the top.
3. Tap the product row to open its detail view, then tap the **Edit** (pencil) icon — or long-press the product row and choose **Edit** from the context menu.
4. Update any of the following fields:
   - **Name**: Change the display name. Old receipts keep the original name.
   - **Selling Price**: New price takes effect immediately on the POS.
   - **Cost Price**: Update when your supplier changes their price.
   - **Stock Quantity**: Correct the count manually if there is a discrepancy.
   - **Category**: Move the product to a different category.
   - **Barcode**: Replace or add a barcode.
   - **Unit**: Change the unit of measure.
   - **Low Stock Alert threshold**: Adjust the warning level.
   - **Product Image**: Tap the image to replace it.
5. Tap **Save** to apply changes.

## Bulk Price Update

If you need to update prices for many products at once:

1. Go to **Products → More (⋮) → Bulk Edit**.
2. Select the products to update.
3. Enter a new price or apply a percentage change.
4. Tap **Apply**.

## Notes

- Price changes are logged with a timestamp. Go to **Product → Price History** to see past prices.
- Stock adjustments from the edit screen are recorded as "Manual Adjustment" in the transaction log.
- Changing the barcode immediately affects POS scanning — make sure the new barcode is correct before saving.
