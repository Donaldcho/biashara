# Business profile for AI agents
feature_id: business_profile
language: en

The **Business profile** teaches Biashara AI agents who you are — not just your sales numbers.

## Why it matters

Agents read your profile before every weekly review, pricing tip, or cash-flow summary. They refer to your business by name, know your target customers, suppliers, and goals, and speak to you as the owner.

## How to set it up

1. Open **Settings** → **Business profile**.
2. Tap **Redo conversation setup** for a friendly Q&A (one question at a time), or edit fields directly.
3. Answer in your language — type or use voice where available.

## What to include

- Business name and what you sell or offer
- Who your customers are
- Location and opening hours
- Main suppliers (e.g. "Mama Grace — hair products, Gikomba")
- Monthly revenue target and growth goals

You can update the profile any time. Agents pick up changes on the next run.
