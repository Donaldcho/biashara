# App Settings
feature_id: settings
language: en

Settings let you configure BiasharaAI to match your business. Go to **Settings** from the main menu (tap the gear icon or profile avatar).

## Business Profile

- **Business Name**: Appears on receipts, statements, and reports. Change it here if you rename your business.
- **Business Type**: Select the closest category (e.g., Retail Shop, Restaurant, Salon, Wholesale). Affects AI recommendations.
- **Location**: Your town or city. Used for regional AI context.
- **Logo**: Upload your business logo — it appears on printed and shared receipts.
- **Phone Number**: Printed on receipts for customer callbacks.

## Currency and Tax

- **Currency**: Select KES (Kenyan Shilling), TZS (Tanzanian Shilling), UGX (Ugandan Shilling), USD, or other supported currencies.
- **Currency Symbol Position**: Before or after the amount.
- **Default Tax Rate**: Enter your VAT rate (e.g., 16% in Kenya). Applied to sales where tax is enabled.
- **Tax Inclusive Pricing**: Toggle on if your displayed prices already include tax; toggle off if tax is added on top.

## Receipt Settings

- **Receipt Header**: Custom text printed at the top of receipts (e.g., "Thank you for shopping with us!").
- **Receipt Footer**: Custom footer message.
- **Show Cost Price on Receipt**: Toggle off to hide cost prices from customer receipts.
- **Default Receipt Action**: Choose what happens after a sale: Print, Share via WhatsApp, or Skip.

## Data and Backup

- **Backup Frequency**: Daily, Weekly, or Manual.
- **Backup Destination**: Local storage or Google Drive (requires sign-in).
- **Restore from Backup**: Load a previous backup if data is lost.

## Language and Display

- **App Language**: Choose from English, Swahili, French, Arabic, Portuguese, Hindi, Chinese.
- **Theme**: Light or Dark mode.
- **Date Format**: DD/MM/YYYY or MM/DD/YYYY.
