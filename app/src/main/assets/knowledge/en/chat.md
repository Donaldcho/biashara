# AI Chat
feature_id: chat
language: en

The Chat screen lets you ask questions about your business in plain language — English or Swahili — and get instant answers powered by the on-device AI model.

## Opening Chat

Tap **Chat** in the bottom navigation bar, or tap the chat bubble icon on the home screen.

## What You Can Ask

The AI has access to your business data and can answer questions such as:

- "What were my total sales today?"
- "How much profit did I make this week?"
- "Which product sold the most last month?"
- "Does Amina owe me money?"
- "What is my current stock of Unga flour?"
- "Show me all M-Pesa payments from yesterday."
- "What are my top 5 best-selling products?"
- "How much have I spent on expenses this month?"

## Asking in Swahili

The AI understands Swahili queries:
- "Mauzo ya leo ni ngapi?"
- "Deni ya John ni kiasi gani?"
- "Bidhaa gani inauzwa zaidi?"

## How Responses Work

- Answers are generated on-device using the downloaded AI model. No internet is needed.
- For data queries, the AI pulls figures directly from your local database — answers are accurate.
- For general business advice, the AI uses its built-in knowledge. Always verify important decisions.

## Conversation History

Your chat history is saved locally. Scroll up to see past conversations. Tap the **New Chat** button to start a fresh session.

## Voice Input in Chat

Tap the **microphone** icon next to the text field to dictate your question instead of typing. See the Voice Commands guide for details.

## Limitations

- The AI cannot make purchases, send money, or access the internet.
- Very complex multi-step calculations may take a few seconds — the on-device model needs processing time.
- If the AI is unsure of an answer, it will say so rather than guess.
