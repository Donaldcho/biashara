# Add a New Product
feature_id: add_product
language: en

Adding a product makes it available in your POS till and inventory. You only need to do this once per product.

## Steps to Add a Product

1. Open the **Products** screen from the bottom navigation bar or the main menu.
2. Tap the **+** button (top-right corner) to open the New Product form.
3. Fill in the product details:
   - **Name** (required): The product name customers will see on receipts.
   - **Selling Price** (required): The price you charge customers per unit.
   - **Cost Price** (optional but recommended): What you pay to buy or make the product. Used for profit calculations.
   - **Stock Quantity**: How many units you currently have. Enter 0 if you manage stock separately.
   - **Category**: Group similar products (e.g., "Beverages", "Stationery"). Tap **New Category** to create one.
   - **Barcode**: Scan a barcode with your camera or type it manually. Used for fast POS scanning.
   - **Unit**: e.g., "piece", "kg", "litre", "dozen".
   - **Low Stock Alert**: Set a threshold — you'll be notified when stock falls below this number.
   - **Product Image**: Tap the camera icon to take a photo or pick from your gallery.

4. Tap **Save** to create the product.

## Tips

- If you sell loose goods (sugar, flour by weight), set the unit to "kg" and leave stock as 0 — record weight at point of sale.
- Products with barcodes can be scanned directly at the POS without searching.
- Adding a cost price unlocks the profit margin view in Reports.
- You can edit any product detail later without affecting past sales records.

## Troubleshooting

- **Barcode not scanning?** Make sure the camera has permission and the barcode is well-lit and flat.
- **Category not showing?** Pull down to refresh the category list or create a new one inline.
