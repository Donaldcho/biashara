# Customers
feature_id: customers
language: en

The Customers section stores your regular buyers. Linking customers to sales enables debt tracking, purchase history, personalised receipts, and credit limits.

## Adding a Customer

1. Go to **Customers** from the bottom navigation bar or main menu.
2. Tap the **+** button.
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** (required)
   - **Phone Number**: Used for WhatsApp receipts and SMS debt reminders.
   - **Email** (optional)
   - **Location / Address** (optional): Useful for delivery businesses.
   - **Credit Limit**: Maximum amount the customer can owe you at one time. Leave blank for no limit.
   - **Notes**: Any extra information (e.g., "Pays on Fridays", "Wholesale buyer").
4. Tap **Save**.

## Viewing a Customer

Tap any customer in the list to open their profile. You will see:
- **Total Purchases**: Lifetime spending.
- **Outstanding Debt**: Current amount owed.
- **Purchase History**: Every sale linked to this customer.
- **Debt History**: Payments made against credit.

## Searching and Filtering

- Use the **Search** bar to find a customer by name or phone number.
- Filter by: All, Has Debt, No Debt, Frequent Buyers.

## Editing a Customer

1. Open the customer profile.
2. Tap the **Edit** (pencil) icon.
3. Update any field and tap **Save**.

## Deleting a Customer

Long-press the customer row and choose **Delete**. Customers with outstanding debt cannot be deleted — clear the debt first. Past sales linked to the customer are preserved.

## Sending a Statement

Open a customer profile, tap **More (⋮) → Send Statement** to send a WhatsApp or SMS summary of their outstanding balance and recent transactions.
